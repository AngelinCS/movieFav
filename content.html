Schindlers List

This film was first introduced to me during High School. I can still remember how it happened, it was during a History class that the my teacher informed us that we will be watching Schindlers-List. At the time I wasn't the best student so my initial reaction was to snicker with my friends in the back of the class. My classmates also held the same disdain as me at the idea of watching a movie called "Schindler", fortunately it didn't take long for all of us to be tranfixed with the amazing story of Schindler and his stuggles to do the right thing in Nazy Germany. The movie was so impactful with its messaging that it let me to reflect on my own actions and strive to improve. I can happily say that I own a copy of this amazing movie in blu-ray . I HIGHLY RECOMMEND

The Day After Tomorrow

I love this movie ever since I was a kid. It really makes you think of the consequences of global heating and also the pontential of us (humanity) self-destroying ourselfs. I rewatch this movie so much since the story telling is always fresh and the story is gripping at an existential level. Highly Recommend.

Jaws

Jaws theirs not much to say about jaws who doesn't like killer sharks. This movie was epic when I first viewed it as a kid, it was a true taste of horror. The man vs animal dynamic in the film was well paced and the story never got stale. Highly Recommend.
